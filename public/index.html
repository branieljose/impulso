How to...create a MIΛ VETRINΛ Inspiration Showroom Design

What are...

    Wall Colors:
  • Color is used to personalize Decor or Floor space.
    Rooms:
  • Are ready made MIΛ VETRINΛ inspiration Showroom designs.
  • Customize your inspiration Room by accessorizing with Furniture and/or Artwork.

    • NOTE: You cannot add wall color to a ready made Room.

    Decors and Floors
  • These spaces allow you to personalize your inspiration.
  • Decors are spaces with some furnishings.
  • Floors are spaces with no furnishings.
    Artwork and Furniture
  • Use to accessorize your inspiration Room, Decor or Floor space.
  • To move, select and drag
  • To resize, hover to a corner and pull one of the square handles.
  • To remove, double click on the Artwork or Furniture then confirm delete.
    Textures
  • Use to add Texture to your Wall Color. This provides depth to your wall.
  • Adding texture will not affect your design.

How to use Rooms...

    Select your Room from the Rooms tab, then Accessorize...
  • Add Furniture
  • Add Artwork

    • NOTE: You cannot add Wall Color.

How to use Floors...

    Select Floor space from the Floors tab, then Customize...
  • Add Wall Color
  • Add Artwork
  • Add Furniture
  • Add Texture

    • NOTE: If you add a Decor space you will automatically replace your Floor space. App will keep your Wall Color, Artwork, Furniture and wall Texture.

How to use Decors...

    Select Decor from the Decors tab, then Customize...
  • Add Wall Color
  • Add Artwork
  • Add Furniture
  • Add Textures

    • NOTE: If you add a Floor space you will automatically replace your Decor space. App will keep your Wall Color, Artwork, Furniture and wall Texture.

How to use Artwork...

    Select Artwork from the Artwork tab, then use to Accessorize you inspiration...
  • Add to Rooms, Decors and Floor spaces
  • To Move select and drag, Resize by hovering to a corner and pulling one of the square handles, Remove by double clicking on the Artwork and confirming delete.

How to use Furniture...

    Select from Furnitue from the Furniture tab, then use to Accessorize your inspiration...
  • Add to Rooms, Decors and Floor spaces
  • To Move select and drag, Resize by hovering to a corner and pulling one of the square handles, Remove by double clicking on the Furniture and confirming delete.

How to use Textures...

    Select Texture from the Textures tab, then use to add texture to your wall.
  • Adding texture will not affect your design.
